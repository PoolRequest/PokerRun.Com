The Rules:

  • Card Stops will open at 11am and close at 4pm.
  • Card Stops can be visited in any order.
  • Upon registration and payment, contestants will be given a registration ticket with a unique ID. This will allow more than one participant per boat.
  • Contestants will present their registration ticket at every Card Stop to receive one playing card in a sealed envelope.
  • Contestants must be in Victoria Marina Harbour/Sunset Grille at 5:30 to register their hand.
  • The card envelopes must remain sealed. Envelopes that are not sealed are disqualified.
  • All hands must be be registered by 6pm.

Extra Cards:

  • You may purchase up to three (3) extra cards when registering.
  • These will be community cards posted at the poker part at Sunset Grille.
  • The community cards will be numbered #1, #2 and #3.
  • If you purchase one (1) extra card, it will be in the #1 position. Two (2) extra cards will be in the #1 and #2 position. Three (3) extra cards gives you all three.

How to Win:

  • The best five(5) card hand wins.
  • In the event of a tie, the winner will be determined by the highest suit.
  • If the hands are exactly the same the prize money will be split.

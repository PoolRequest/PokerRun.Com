Saturday July 9th

11am-4pm Card Stops

Poker Party and Awards 5:30pm-8:00pm at Sunset Grille

The Poker Run:

Participants will collect a card from each of the participating marinas in any order they choose, from 11am-4pm. Each participating Marina will have signs or large balloons directing participants to the card pick up. All cards must remain sealed in the envelopes.

The Poker Run Blast Off:

11am at Victoria Harbor Marina. Boats can start gathering at 10:30am if they would like to join for this.

The Poker Party:

Participants and sponsors will gather at Sunset Grille for the Poker Party after the run. There will be a booth to register the cards, and food, live music, vendors and prizes! All participants must arrive no later than 5:30pm at Sunset Grille, in order for all cards to be registered no later than 6pm. Please see complete rules and registration details.

New This Year: Poker Run T-shirts will be available for purchase at the Captain's Meeting and the Poker Party! All proceeds benefit Folds of Honor

The Prizes:

1st Place: $1500

2nd Place: $500

3rd Place: 65 Gallon Orion Cooler
